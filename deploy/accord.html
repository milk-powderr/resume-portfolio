  • About
  • Accord is a chocolate brand built around a single idea: the customer decides. The name comes from the verb (to grant power or authority) and that's exactly what the product does, letting buyers set their own ratio of chocolate to inclusions across three pull-out shelves. The packaging is a cube with three sliding shelves, each embedded with small diamond indents that change color to reflect the ratio selected for that layerThe customization happens through the accompanying website, where customers configure their order before it's fulfilled. The physical prototype was built using a laser cutter.
