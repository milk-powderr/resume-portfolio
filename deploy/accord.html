  • About
  • Accord is a brand of chocolate that not only offers regular chocolates but lets you decide how much chocolate inclusions come along with it.
  • Process
  • The word 'accord' when used as a verb means 'to grant power or authority.' 'accord' was created with the purpose of giving 'power' to the customer, and in this case it's the power to chose the amount of chocolate inclusions in your desired product. The featured version is the cookies & cream edition. The logo reclects the overall design of the box, taking the different ratios a customer might order in consideration. Each box contains three sections, each section holding nine cubes of chocolate. The addition of ribbons as tabs help give the package a finishing luxury touch.
