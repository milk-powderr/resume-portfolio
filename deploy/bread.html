  • Objective
  • To create an animated menu bar and a toggle button.
  • About
  • Features like toggle switches or menu bars are essential to programs such as applications and websites, but they can become so much more when character is given to them.
  • Process
  • I decided to create a toggle switch that resembled a baking hot cross bun. The bun goes through the motion of being baked to represent the 'on/off' function. For the menu bar, I designed a menu bar for an application belonging to a pastry store. I designed it to look like a square-shaped cake that had the layers exposed. The three berries falling from the top add variety to the design.
