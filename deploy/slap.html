  • Slap
  • Product / Packaging Design
  • Photoshop, Illustrator, Figma
  • Objective
  • To create a package and product design for a canned drink.
  • About
  • Slap is a citrus-flavored energy drink. This project is a 4-pack that contains four different flavors: lemon, key lime, mandarin, and grapefruit. The name "Slap" is attributed to the bite of sourness from citrus fruits that is akin to getting slapped in the face. The die line of the package is a single sheet, making it easy for assembly and for commercial shipping in large amounts. The bright and punchu colors were chosen to make it stand out on a shelf.
