  • Charisma House Website Redesign
  • Web Design
  • Webflow, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • About
  • Charisma House is a Japanese music multimedia project by King Records. This is a full redesign of their web presence, built in Webflow with custom JavaScript for interactions Webflow doesn't natively support.
  • The design started with low-fidelity Figma wireframes across desktop, tablet, and mobile before moving into Webflow. Bold colors and eye-catching typography were chosen to match the project's flamboyant, spontaneous energy — staying faithful to the existing brand while giving it a completely new visual register.
  • The site includes a landing page, individual character profiles with introductory voice lines, a news page, episode releases, a merch store link, and a live Twitter feed.
