  • Video games are one of the most popular forms of entertainment in the modern world, from as far back as Pac-Man to newer releases like Genshin Impact. But have we taken time out to study how they affect us psychologically? People have different reasons for gaming: from letting steam off to simply enjoying a virtual space with friends. This issue of this magazine gives its readers a closer look at the possible effects that this hobby may have on them, be it positive or negative.
