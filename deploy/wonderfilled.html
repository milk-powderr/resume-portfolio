  • About
  • OREO is one of the most recognizable cookie brands in America if not the whole world. The OREO cookie comes in many flavors and sizes. Sometimes they're covered in chocolate, come in the form of an ice cream, and have even been adapted into drinks. At the end of the day, it all comes down to the original humble cookie: white cream sandwiched by two charcoal-colored disks.
  • Process
  • This animation was created in accordance to OREO's brand style guides. All assets of this project were made in Figma and the animation was created using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and GSAP.
