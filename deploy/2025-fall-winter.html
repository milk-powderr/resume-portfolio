  • About
  • Accord is a brand of chocolate that not only offers regular chocolates but lets you decide how much chocolate inclusions come along with it.
  • Process
  • The word 'accord' when used as a verb means 'to grant power or authority.' Accord was created to give 'power' to the customer, and in this case, it's the power to choose the amount of chocolate inclusions in your desired product. The featured version is the cookies & cream edition. Each box contains three sections, each section holding nine cubes of chocolate. The addition of ribbons as tabs gives the package a luxury touch.
