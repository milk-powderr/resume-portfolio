  • Unique Industries, Inc. / 2025 Fall-Winter
  • Product/Packaging Design
  • Illustrator, Large-Format Printer, Cricut Cutting Machine, Heat Sealing Machine, X-Acto Knife
  • About
  • This project covered party supplies under Unique Industries, Inc.'s own brand for Halloween and Christmas in 2025 Fall-Winter. I built mock-ups of plates, napkins, and packages.
  • Process
  • I received the files from the design team and prepared them for print in Illustrator before sending them to the large-format color printer. I then applied the appropriate adhesives to adhere them to weighted paper. Some of the products required reactive foil, so I extracted the necessary SVGs from the print files and printed out a toner before running them through the foiling machine. I then paired the mocked-up products with the appropriate mocked-up packages.
