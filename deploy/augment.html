  • Objective
  • To create a new brand identity for the public garden 'Bartram's Garden'.
  • About
  • Bartram's Garden is the oldest survivng botanical garden in North America. It is located in Southwest Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. The aim of this project is to give it a new brand identity, encluding but not limited to a new logo, website, merchandise and more.
  • Process
  • The goal of this project was to give Bartram's Garden a more modern and upbeat identity. Since Bartram's Garden is involved with horticulture and apiculture, those two elements were taken into consideration when designing a new logo. The hexagon, flower and leaf motifs were used repeatedly in the new brand identity. Items such as flyers, merchandise and environmental branding were included to enhance the authenticity of the design.
