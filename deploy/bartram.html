  • Bartram's Garden Rebrand
  • Brand Identity
  • Illustrator, Photoshop, Figma, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, GSAP
  • Objective
  • To create a new brand identity for Bartram's Garden.
  • About
  • Bartram's Garden is the oldest surviving botanical garden in North America. It's located in Southwest Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. Bartram's Garden doesn't operate under horticulture and agriculture alone, they also involve themselves in apiculture, an area that isn't strongly represented across their brand, so my main goal was to draw attention to it.
  • Process
  • Since Bartram's Garden is involved in agriculture, horticulture, and apiculture, those three elements were taken into consideration when designing a new logo. The hexagonal leaf, flower, and honeycomb cell motifs are used repeatedly throughout the brand. Items such as flyers, merchandise, and environmental branding are included to remind viewers of Bartram's Garden's main areas of operation. The logo animation was made with code (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and GSAP), while the logo and other elements were developed in Illustrator.
