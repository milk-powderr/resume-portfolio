  • Unique Industries, Inc. / 2026 Spring-Summer
  • Product + Package
  • Illustrator, Large-Format Printer, Cricut, Heat Sealing Machine, X-Acto Knife
  • About
  • This project covered party supplies under Unique Industries, Inc.'s own brand for occasions in 2026 Spring-Summer. This includes Valentine's Day, Easter, Religious, Spring, Summer, Graduation and Patriotic. I built mock-ups of plates, napkins, placemats, banners, and packages.
  • Process
  • I received the files from the design team and prepared them for print in Illustrator before sending them to the large-format color printer. I then applied the appropriate adhesives to adhere them to weighted paper before cutting them out and mocking them up. Some of the products required foil, so I extracted the necessary SVGs from the print files before sending them to the Cricut cutting machine. I weeded and applied the foil as needed before pairing the mocked-up products with the appropriate mocked-up packages.
