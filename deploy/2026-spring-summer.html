  • Unique Industries, Inc. / 2026 Spring - Summer
  • Product + Packaging
  • Illustrator, Large Format Printer, Cricut, X-Acto Knife
  • About
  • This project covered party supplies under Unique Industries, Inc.'s own brand for occassions in 2026 Spring - Summer. This includes Valentine's Day, Easter, Religious, Spring, Summer, Graduation and Patriotic. I built mockups of plates, napkins, placemeats, and banners.
  • Process
  • I recieved the files from the design team and prepared them for print in Illustrator before sending them to the large format color printer. I then applied the appropriate adhesives to adhere them to weighted paper. Some of the products required foil, so I extracted the necessary SVGs from the print files before sending them to the Cricut machine. I weeded and applied the foil as needed before pairing the packages with the appropriate products.
