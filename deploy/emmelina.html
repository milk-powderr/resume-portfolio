  • Emmelina
  • Brand Identity
  • Photoshop, Figma, CapCut
  • Objective
  • To create a logo for a milk comapny.
  • About
  • Emmelina is a dairy company that produces and distributes bottled milk.
  • Process
  • The colors blue and yellow are were chosen for the logo because they're frequently associated with dairy products: yellow from milk carotenoids and blue from the tinge from scattered light produced in milk particles. The logo depicts the face of a cow surrounded by the word "Emmelina". The patterns at the back of the business cards bring some excitement to the otherwise simple logo.
